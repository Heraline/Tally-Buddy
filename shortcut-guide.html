How it works

📸
Take a screenshot

After any payment — GrabPay, Touch 'n Go, Maybank, food delivery receipt — press Side + Volume Up

⚡
Tap "Log Expense"

Run from your home screen or widget. Gemini AI reads the screenshot and extracts amount, merchant, category, date & time.

💳
Tally Buddy opens pre-filled

The form auto-fills with all details extracted from the receipt. Just tap Save!

Make it even faster

👆
Back Tap (fastest!)

Settings → Accessibility → Touch → Back Tap → Double Tap → assign "Log Expense"

🎛️
Control Center (iOS 18+)

Settings → Control Center → add your shortcut. One swipe + one tap from anywhere.

🔆
Action Button (iPhone 15 Pro+)

Settings → Action Button → Shortcuts → "Log Expense"
