How it works

1
Take a screenshot of any payment

GrabPay, Touch 'n Go, Maybank, DuitNow, food delivery — anything showing an amount & merchant. Press Side + Volume Up.

2
Tap "Log Expense" shortcut

Run from your home screen, widget, or Back Tap. Gemini AI reads the screenshot and extracts all transaction details automatically.

3
Family Wallet opens pre-filled

Amount, merchant, category, date & time — all extracted from the receipt. Just review and tap Save ✓
